General Policy

AngMiraeup Skillpath Services primarily provides information, documentation assistance, digital guidance and customer support services.

Service Charges

Any service charges, if applicable, will be clearly communicated before the service begins.

Refund Eligibility

Refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. A refund may be considered only if a payment was made incorrectly or a service could not be delivered due to our own error.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after service has started.
  • Incomplete or incorrect information provided by the user.
  • Rejection or delay by third-party organizations.
  • Failure to meet eligibility requirements.
  • Outcomes beyond our control.

Refund Request

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please contact our support team with complete payment details and your reason for the request.

Processing Time

Approved refunds, where applicable, are generally processed within 7–14 business days.
